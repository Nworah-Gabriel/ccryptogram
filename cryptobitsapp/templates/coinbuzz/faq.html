Next, add your personal details. Complete the requested form, adding your address, phone number, and date of birth.
Coinmama: add your personal details

Finally, upload your documents. Choose the ID type you want to upload, based on your country of residence. Photograph the front and back with your cell phone camera, making sure all four corners are visible, and that both the image and text are clear, then upload them to your account.

Coinmama: Upload documents Next, write “Coinmama” and today’s date on a piece of paper. Holding the paper and the ID you’ve just uploaded, take a photograph of yourself. Make sure your face, ID, and paper note are all clearly visible. Then upload the photo to your account.

What are binary options?.

Binary options is a derivative financial instruments, the peculiarity of which is in a fixed-income and risk. A trader must determine whether the price of the underlying asset is above or below the current (it is called “the strike price”). You can receive up to 90% per trade. More precise information on the payout can be found in each option. If the option is unprofitable (i.e., your prediction was incorrect), you can lose 100% of the investment. The amount of profit per option does not depend on the number of points that change the value of the underlying asset.
